Instructors

Our yoga instructors at Zenspot share the vision of providing the best experience reachable during your retreat. They have set up selected times throughout the trip to include our yoga sessions. Most of these sessions will be held outdoors, but we have some indoor and coaching yoga sessions where you can learn certain skills to take home with you.
